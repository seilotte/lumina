<h1 align = "center">
    Sei Biome Colours
</h1>

<p align = "center">
    This datapack modifies biome colours.
</p>

## Installation

1. Download the `sei_biome_colours` folder.
1. Open your Minecraft `.jar` file (`.minecraft/versions/x.x.x.jar`) and navigate to `data/minecraft/worldgen/biome`.
1. Copy its contents into `sei_biome_colours/data/minecraft/worldgen/biome`.
1. Run `_patch_biomes.py` **in place**.
1. Optionally, delete `_patch_biomes.py`.
1. Move `sei_biome_colours` into your world's `datapacks` folder.

> [!NOTE]
> Edit `_patch_biomes.py` to customize biome colours.<br>
> For more information, refer to the [Minecraft Wiki](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Biome#List_of_biome_climates).

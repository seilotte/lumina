<h1 align = "center">
    Sei Biome Colours
</h1>

<p align = "center">
    This datapack modifies biome colours.
</p>

## Installation

1. Download the `sei_biome_colours` folder.
1. Locate the `biomes_colours.txt` file and move it to `sei_biome_colours/data/minecraft/worldgen/biome`.
1. Navigate to your Minecraft version folder: `.minecraft/versions/x.x.x.jar`.
1. Open the `.jar` file and go to `x.x.x.jar/data/minecraft/worldgen/biome`.
1. Copy all the contents to `sei_biome_colours/data/minecraft/worldgen/biome`.
1. Locate the `biomes_colours_jsons.py` script.
1. Open the script, specify the path to the datapack's *biome* directory, execute it.
1. Optionally, delete `biomes_colours.txt` and `biomes_colours_jsons.py`. 
1. Move the `sei_biome_colours` folder into your world's `datapacks` directory.

> [!WARNING]
> The python script may fail; formatting is important.

> [!NOTE]
> To customize your biome colours, modify `biomes_colours.txt`.
For more information, refer to the [Minecraft Wiki](https://minecraft.wiki).
